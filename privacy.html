PERMISSIONS ARE REQUIRED

SMS related permissions
We collect, monitor and transmit your SMS data which helps us in identifying the various bank accounts that you may be holding, cash flow patterns, description and amount of the transactions. This data is used for the purpose of performing credit risk assessment. This credit risk assessment enables us to perform a quicker loan disbursal and identify any fraudulent activity and strengthen data security. This data may be collected even when the app is not in use.

Microphone related permissions
Permission for microphone is required to initiate two-way audio communication as a part of KYC journey. This may be stored for future verification purpose by us, our service providers, affiliates and partners.

Storage related permissions
Storage permission is required so that your KYC and other relevant documents can be securely downloaded and saved to your phone. These KYC documents can then be easily uploaded for faster completion of your loan application and disbursal. This will help us to ensure that you undergo a seamless experience on our app.

Location related permissions
Location related permissions are required to assess serviceability of your loan application. This will be used in verifying the address and undertaking continuous credit risk assessment. It will expedite complete of your KYC process along with facilitating better credit access. It will also help in strengthening security against any unauthorized access to your account. This permission will also be used for providing better customer experience and providing customized offers.

Phone related permissions
Phone related permissions is required of specific information that is collected and monitored by our app regarding your device including your hardware model, build model, RAM, storage, Unique device identifiers like IMEI, serial number, SSAID, SIM information that includes network operators, roaming state, MNC and MCC codes, WIFI information that includes MAC address and mobile network information so as to uniquely identify the services and to ensure that no unauthorized device acts on your behalf which will help us in preventing frauds. This will also help us in enriching your credit profile and assessing your credit worthiness

Camera related permissions
Permissions to access Camera is required so that requisite KYC documents can be easily scanned or captured and thus saving time by allowing us to auto-fill relevant data so that an audio/video call can be initiated for the purpose of KYC completion. This will ensure that you are provided with a seamless experience while using our application.

Installed Application related permissions
This installed Application related permission is required as we collect each installed applications metadata information which includes the application name, package name, installed time, updated time, version name and version code of each installed application on your device to assess your credit worthiness and provide you with pre-approved customized loan offers.

User Personal Information related permissions
This personal information related permissions is required to collect user account data which includes email address and user public profile information like name, photo, ASID depending on the social media or networking platform used by you (such as Google, Facebook, etc.) to log-in to the app. This information is required as a part of registration process and to access our service and is also used to auto-populate relevant fields in the course of our app interface. Our app also collects mobile number for verification to check the active SIM status of the device, uniquely identify yo9u and to prevent frauds and unauthorize access.

Please not that few data points collected here are mandatory for building comprehensive credit risk assessment and credit profile and thus shall be securely shared with our registered service providers, affiliates and partners.
